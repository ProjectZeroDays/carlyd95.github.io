A little about myself...

  • Hey, my name is Carlton. I initially made this website to use as a cydia repo but my interests have overall changed over the past few years. I am now using it as a fun way to teach myself HTML & CSS etc. while also making use of it to sell seeds that I have harvested from my collection of plants along with extra plants or seed grown plants, primarily cacti and african succulents. I also plan to use this site as a way to share with other plant enthusiasts certain things i have learned and resources I have used. I too will be using this website to host my homebrew recipes to share with homebrewers and others alike.
