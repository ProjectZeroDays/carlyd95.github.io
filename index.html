Introduction

Hey, my name is Carlton. And put quite simply... I. Love. Plants! Particularly Cacti and African succulents... My favorite genera include: Haworthia, Tulista, Haworthiospsis, Gasteria, Lithops and similar Mesembs, Stapeliads, certain Euphorbia, Adromischus, Certain Cacti and some caudiciforms and bulbs. I created this site in order to share, the plants that I love so much, with others while at the same time earning some extra cash to invest in more plants and seeds to enrichen my own collection.
