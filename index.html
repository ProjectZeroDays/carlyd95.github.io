Introduction

Hey, my name is Carlton. And put quite simply... I. Love. Plants. Some beloved genera include: Haworthia, Tulista, Haworthiospsis, Gasteria, Bulbine, Lithops, Stapeliads, Euphorbia, Adromischus, Ariocarpus, Astrophytum, Gymnocalycium, Lophophora, Trichocereus. I intend to use this website as an avenue to share with others the opportunity to grow the plants that I find so interesting.
