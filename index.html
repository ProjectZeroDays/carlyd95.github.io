Introduction

  • Hey, my name is Carlton. I. Love. Plants. Particularly cacti and African succulents... My favorite genera include: haworthia, tulista, haworthiospsis, gasteria, lithops, stapeliads, certain euphorbia, adromischus, certain cacti and some caudiciforms and bulbs. I created this site in order to share, the plants that I love so much, with others while at the same time earning some extra cash to invest in more plants and seeds to enrichen my own collection.
